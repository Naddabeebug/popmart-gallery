The other one series

The Ghost

The Presence of Absence

A soul caught between realms, The Ghost represents the longing for recognition and the pain of invisibility. It speaks to those who feel unheard, unseen, and detached, existing on the fringes of their own lives.

Staring

The Weight of Perception

Who is watching? Staring evokes the anxiety of scrutiny, the fear of judgment, and the eerie sensation of being both observed and unknown. It reflects the discomfort of being truly seen.

The Fox

The Trickster’s Wisdom

Deception and wisdom are two sides of the same coin. The Fox embodies the duality of cleverness—sometimes it is a tool for survival, sometimes a mask to hide one’s true self.

Vagrancy

The Endless Search

Wandering without direction, Vagrancy reflects the human condition of seeking purpose in an indifferent world. It represents the struggle of existence, where the traveler moves forward yet never arrives, mirroring the eternal search for belonging.

Amnesia

The Fragility of Memory

Memory shapes identity, but what happens when it fades? Amnesia represents the fear of losing oneself, of forgetting what makes us who we are. It speaks to the quiet terror of erasure.

The Monster

The Shadow Self

Every person carries a monster inside—a collection of fears, regrets, and repressed desires. This figure embodies the Jungian concept of the shadow self, the parts of us we hide yet cannot escape.

The Crow

The Survivor’s Instinct

A symbol of intelligence and adaptation, The Crow represents resilience in the face of hardship. It is a reminder that sometimes, survival requires cunning and an understanding of life’s darkest truths

Nowhere Safe

The Illusion of Security

This figure embodies vulnerability in an unpredictable world. It forces the realization that safety is often a mirage—people build walls to protect themselves, yet true security is always just out of reach.

Cuckoo

The cuckoo bird is both a trickster and an outsider, a creature that thrives in unpredictability. This figure symbolizes the chaos of creativity and the defiance of societal norms, embodying the spirit of those who refuse to conform.
