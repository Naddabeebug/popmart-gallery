Mime Series

Guardian

The Silent Protector

Embodies the role of an unseen sentinel, safeguarding inner vulnerabilities. This figure reflects the strength required to shield oneself and others from emotional harm.

Blind

The Unseen Truth

Represents the concept of willful ignorance or unseen realities. It challenges us to acknowledge the truths we often overlook or choose to ignore.

Seeker

The Face Bearer

Represents the masks we wear to fit into societal norms. It challenges us to look beyond appearances and question the authenticity of the personas we present.

Devilry

The

Captures the essence of playful mischief and the allure of bending rules. This figure reminds us of the inner trickster that tempts us toward harmless rebellion.

Drifter

The Wandering Minds

Depicts a state of aimlessness and the search for direction. It reflects the periods in life when one feels unanchored, drifting through experiences in search of purpose.
